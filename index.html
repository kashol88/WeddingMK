Viktig Information

Dress Code

Festive summer attire! Think colorful and comfortable clothing suitable for a garden celebration. The event will take place outdoors in our circus tents, so choose appropriate footwear.

Accommodation

For our out-of-town guests, we recommend booking accommodations in Harplinge or nearby Halmstad. A list of recommended hotels and B&Bs will be provided upon RSVP.

Gifts

Your presence is our greatest gift! However, if you wish to give something, we will provide gift suggestions upon RSVP. We appreciate your thoughtfulness.

Contact

Questions about the celebration? Our toastmaster will be happy to help! Contact details will be shared after your RSVP confirmation.
